How many emails are sent worldwide in one day? ›
English: I will estimate the global population (around 8 billion), assume 50% use email, and that each person sends about 10 emails a day. Then, multiply these numbers together.
Hinglish: Global population (8 billion) ko dhyan mein rakhte hue, main maanunga ki 50% log email use karte hain aur har ek person 10 emails daily bhejta hai. Phir inn numbers ko multiply karke estimate karunga.
Q
How many basketballs and volleyballs can fit inside a Boeing 747? ›
English: I will first estimate the cargo space of a Boeing 747 (around 1700 cubic meters) and then calculate the volume of each basketball (0.007 cubic meters) and volleyball (0.004 cubic meters). Finally, divide the cargo volume by the volume of each ball to estimate how many can fit.
Hinglish: Boeing 747 ki cargo space (1700 cubic meters) ko estimate karne ke baad, main har basketball (0.007 cubic meters) aur volleyball (0.004 cubic meters) ka volume calculate karunga aur phir cargo volume ko divide karunga.

Hinglish: India ki population (1.4 billion) ko dhyan mein rakhte hue, main maanunga ki 70% log daily chai peete hain aur har ek person average 2 cups chai peeta hai. Phir inn numbers ko multiply karunga.
